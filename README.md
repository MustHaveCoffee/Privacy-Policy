Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 22, 2026

App Name: Railroad-Timebook

Developer: Richard Davis

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. Railroad-Timebook (“the App”) does not collect, transmit, or share user data with the developer or any third parties. Any data created within the App is stored solely in the user’s personal iCloud account using Apple’s iCloud services.
Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal or usage data.

Specifically:

We do not collect personal information (such as name, email, phone number, or location)

We do not collect analytics or usage statistics

We do not track users

We do not access device identifiers

We do not log IP addresses

iCloud Data Storage

The App uses Apple iCloud to store user-generated data.
All data is stored only in the user’s own iCloud account
Data is associated with the user’s Apple ID and managed by Apple
The developer does not have access to this data
The data is not transmitted to any developer-owned or third-party servers
Users may manage or delete this data at any time through their iCloud settings.

Third-Party Services

Railroad-Timebook does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.
The only external service used is Apple iCloud, which is provided and secured by Apple under Apple’s own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

No data is shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to the developer or any third parties. Since all data remains within the user’s personal iCloud account, there is no data available for sharing.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. All data stored in iCloud is created and controlled by the user and remains private to their Apple ID.

Security

Data stored in iCloud is protected by Apple’s security and encryption mechanisms. The developer does not directly handle, store, or transmit user data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be reflected by updating the effective date and making the revised version available within the App or its App Store listing.
